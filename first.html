About The Founders!!

Ugobest music worldwide was founded on the 21st of may 2005 by Mr.Olatunji Adebayo(on th left) & Mr.Tilako Dateh(on the right), The record label has nested the most successful afro artists worldwide and we are still recruiting more talents.
